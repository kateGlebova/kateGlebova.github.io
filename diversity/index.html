Diversity as European Union's driving force

The official motto of the European Union is "United in diversity".

Why?

Diversity is in the core of the European project

This motto was chosen as a symbol of Europeans with different backgrounds forming the union to build a prosperous future together while preserving their cultures, traditions and languages. However, not only does cultural diversity pushes the EU forward nowadays.

More!

Diversity Forms

Gender

Gender diversity leads to greater profitability, more innovation, better problem solving and even a boost in opportunity for future women in business.

Age

Having age diverse people, working for the same company, for example, increases the range of skills set, creates opportunities for mentoring, teaching, sharing and learning from one another.

Culture

We learn from people who have different experiences, beliefs and perspectives on things. “Diversity enriches the educational experience”(“On the Importance of Diversity in Higher Education”).

Disability

People with disabilities may just have the skills and competencies you require within your organization yet they are often under-employed. I
